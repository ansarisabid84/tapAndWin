Tab & Win Play
How to Play?
  • Two players are assigned specific sections on the screen.
  • Tap or click within your designated area to start filling it. The game begins when both players tap their sections.
  • Continuously tap within your assigned area to fill it with color or pattern.
  • Both players play simultaneously, racing to fill their respective boxes first.
  • The first player to completely fill their box is the winner.
